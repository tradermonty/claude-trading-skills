## Report Quality Standards

- Quantify all price movements with specific percentages and basis points
- Cite sources for major claims
- Distinguish correlation from causation; acknowledge attribution uncertainty
- Use proper financial terminology consistently
- Objective, fact-based analysis only (no speculation beyond probability-weighted scenarios)
