### Escalation Triggers

**Escalate to Project Sponsor when:**
- ☐ New critical risk identified (score >0.25)
- ☐ Existing risk escalates to critical
- ☐ Mitigation budget exceeded by >10%
- ☐ Multiple high risks threaten project viability
- ☐ Risk requires scope/schedule/budget baseline change
- ☐ External dependencies at risk
- ☐ Stakeholder decision required

**Escalation Process:**
1. Document risk and impact analysis
2. Prepare options with pros/cons
3. Provide recommendation
4. Schedule decision meeting within [X] days
5. Document decision and communicate
6. Update project plan
